Travel period and booking window

Ideal travel period for Raja Ampat

  • Good overall conditions: October to April.
  • Generally calmer waves: November to February.
  • Programs always depend on local weather and sea conditions.

Recommended booking period

  • Book at least 30 - 60 days before departure for better flight options.
  • Earlier booking helps secure homestay and boat capacity for your group.
  • Campus groups can use the lead time to arrange savings, fundraising, or proposal submission.

Sample itinerary 5D4N
Day 1 - Arrival and welcome session

Arrival at Sorong, boat transfer to Raja Ampat, check in at local homestay, welcome briefing, and free time to explore the surrounding pier or beach. Evening is reserved for group introduction and rest.

Day 2 - Piaynemo viewpoint and lagoon

Boat trip to Piaynemo, short hike to the viewpoint, panorama and photo session, lagoon exploration, and snorkeling in selected clear water spots. Return to homestay before sunset.

Day 3 - Snorkeling and sand bank experience

Full day dedicated to snorkeling at coral reef locations and enjoying a sand bank session for content ready group photos. Free time in the evening to rest or share experiences with the group.

Day 4 - Village visit and flexible time

Visit to a local village to see daily life and culture up close. The remaining time is flexible for light walks, additional photography, or relaxing around the homestay.

Day 5 - Check out and return flight

Check out from homestay, boat transfer back to Sorong, then flight to home city. Simple closing session and group photo before departure.

Itinerary can be adjusted by the local team according to weather, safety considerations, and flight schedules, while keeping the main highlights of Raja Ampat.

Important notes, terms, and policies

  • Schedule may change depending on weather and sea conditions.
  • Participants are expected to follow safety instructions for all water activities.
  • Using life jackets during boat rides and snorkeling is strongly recommended.
  • Campus groups are encouraged to appoint a coordinator to communicate with UNITour.

General terms

  • Trip dates and price depend on the availability of accommodation, boats, and flights at the time of booking.
  • A deposit is required to secure the group slot. Remaining payment will follow an agreed schedule.
  • Participant data and copies of ID or passport may be needed for ticketing and local registration.
  • Program can be adjusted if required by safety or operational considerations.

Refund and reschedule policy

  • Refund and reschedule follow the policy of airlines and local operators involved in the trip.
  • Deposit is generally non refundable but can be transferred to another participant from the same group, subject to approval.
  • Reschedule requests will be handled based on seat, homestay, and boat availability.
